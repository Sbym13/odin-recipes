Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a delicous Brazillian dessert that's similar to bonbons (but better). It's one of the most loved things in
Brazil, matter of fact almost every party has it.

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) Can sweetened condensed milk
  • 3 Tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 Tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Combine condensed milk, cocoa, and butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir until
    thickened, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and let rest until mixture is cool enough to handle.
  2. Use your hands to shape into small balls and place on a serving plate. Can be eaten at once or chilled until
    serving.
